Products we've shipped

Healthcare IoT

Connected Healthcare Gateway

Cellular-connected hub that pairs with medical devices over Bluetooth and sends patient data to the cloud. We built the firmware, the security layer, and the manufacturing tooling. Zero vulnerabilities in pen testing.

  • 65,000+ units deployed and growing
  • 10+ medical device types integrated
  • 30x faster factory floor production with custom automation tooling

Transit Analytics

Rail Condition Analytics Platform

Web platform that processes sensor data from rail networks and turns it into maintenance insights. Used by some of the largest transit agencies in North America.

  • 10+ agencies, 5+ years in continuous operation
  • Cut analysis turnaround from weeks to minutes

Predictive Maintenance

RailSense

Custom IoT hardware deployed across multiple transit agencies. Captures ride vibration data in real time and flags track conditions before they become problems.

  • Custom sensor hardware, deployed on live rail
  • Real-time data capture during regular passenger service

Process

How we work

You get something that works, not a prototype that needs to be rewritten.

01

Discovery

Understand the problem first. Define requirements, identify risks, figure out the architecture. Then a fixed-scope proposal with clear milestones.

02

Architecture

Hard technical decisions up front. Platform, protocols, security model, cloud architecture. Get these right and the build goes fast.

03

Build

Focused sprints with regular check-ins. Working hardware and software throughout. TDD and automated testing from day one.

04

Deliver

Production firmware with OTA. Manufacturing tooling. Test frameworks. Documentation. Knowledge transfer. Maintenance agreements.

About

The name

Avrah

Aramaic · to create, to build

In ancient Aramaic, avra k'davra means "I create as I speak." It's where the word abracadabra comes from. The idea that saying something can make it real.

We took the first word. We don't just talk about products. We create them. A conversation becomes a concept, a concept becomes a device in the field. That's the magic.
