§02.0

RailView project

“One dashboard for every segment of track.”

fig. 0 — app screenshot

What

Data management dashboard and grinding-program visualization for railway maintenance. Built for REDACTED. Map-based views, trend analysis, and reporting for transit agencies across North America.

Why

Rail networks generate enormous amounts of condition data. RailView gives maintenance teams the tools to actually use it — prioritize work, visualize grinding programs, and catch problems before they become failures.

§02.1

OpenSpot project

“Parking, made easy.”

fig. 1 — OpenSpot title card

What

Two-sided smart parking. Real-time occupancy for lot operators, spot-finding and reservations for drivers. Magnetometer or camera-based sensing, a dashboard for operators, and an app for everyone else.

Why

Parking is an infrastructure problem dressed up as a convenience problem. The wedge is municipal, hospital, and private lots — places where better data means real money saved and fewer cars circling blocks.

§02.2

Connected Gateway project

“Bluetooth to cellular, secured end-to-end.”

fig. 2 — system overview

What

A Bluetooth-to-cellular gateway for medical sensors — from thermometers and weight scales to ventilators. 65,000+ units deployed. End-to-end encrypted with full device management and FOTA capability. Passed independent penetration testing. Built for REDACTED.

Role

Embedded software, device connectivity, cloud integration. Worked across the full stack from firmware on the gateway hardware to the cloud infrastructure receiving and routing device data.

§02.3

RailSense project

Continuous rail defect monitoring from revenue service vehicles.

fig. 3 — system overview

What

Undercar noise and vibration sensors for transit railways. Custom hardware, AI-assisted maintenance prioritization. Every revenue service vehicle becomes a continuous track inspector.

Why

Dedicated inspection vehicles are expensive and run on schedules. RailSense monitors track condition during normal passenger service — continuous data, no operational disruption, problems caught earlier.
